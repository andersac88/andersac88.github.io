Résumé

My name is Andrew Andersen and I am 30 years old. I currently reside in Arlington Heights with my fiancée, Ashley, our two cats, Maggie and Millie, and our miniature pincher, Mia.

I am a graduate of Miami University in Oxford Ohio and went to university with the intention of undertaking a career in medicine however towards the end of my tenure I realized I wasn't destined to be a doctor.

Following graduation, still unsure of my purpose, I followed in the footsteps of my father and began working in property management. While this path has afforded me a comfortable lifestyle I find that the field lacks a certain mental stimulation that I desire.

Ever eager to learn new things, I am fortunate enough to have come across Northwestern's Coding Bootcamp. Unlike other programs, Northwestern has permitted me to learn a new skill set on a part—time basis.

While it would be nice if this class ultimately leads me to a new more fulfilling career, I am excited to learn regardless.
